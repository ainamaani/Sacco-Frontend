About Us

Welcome to Kimwanyi SACCO! We are a trusted Saving and Credit Cooperative Organization dedicated to empowering our members and promoting financial well-being.
With a strong commitment to member satisfaction, we provide a wide range of financial services, including savings accounts, loans, and investment opportunities. Our team of experienced professionals is here to support you on your financial journey, offering personalized solutions and expert guidance.
At Kimwanyi SACCO, we prioritize transparency, security, and community development. Join us today and experience the difference of being part of a cooperative that puts your needs first.

Member eligibility

At Kimwanyi SACCO, becoming a member is open to individuals aged 18 and above residing in the local community. To be eligible, prospective members must provide valid identification and agree to comply with SACCO policies.
Shareholding is required, and the membership application process involves completing an application form. Compliance with regulatory requirements, such as AML and KYC guidelines, is essential. Join our SACCO to access a wide range of financial services, benefit from personalized solutions, and contribute to the economic growth of our community.
